About SpecimenPro Events

Discover the exciting world of virtual mineral specimen collection

What Are SpecimenPro Events?

SpecimenPro Events are interactive, location-based experiences where participants can discover and collect virtual mineral specimens using our mobile app. These events combine education, exploration, and gamification to create an engaging learning experience about geology and mineralogy.

Whether you're at a museum, science center, geological site, or educational institution, SpecimenPro Events bring the fascinating world of minerals to life through cutting-edge augmented reality and QR code technology.

  • Educational and entertaining for all ages
  • Hands-on learning about geology and mineralogy
  • Interactive specimen collection and identification
  • Badge system for achievements and progression
  • Perfect for schools, museums, and educational institutions

How It Works

Getting started with SpecimenPro Events is simple and intuitive. Our system is designed to be accessible while providing a rich, educational experience.

  1. Download the SpecimenPro App - Available on iOS, our app provides the tools you need to scan QR codes and manage your specimen collection.
  2. Find an Event - Look for SpecimenPro events at participating museums, schools, or educational venues near you.
  3. Scan QR Codes - Throughout the event venue, you'll find QR codes placed near exhibits, displays, or marked geological features.
  4. Collect Specimens - Each QR code reveals a virtual mineral specimen with detailed information about its properties, formation, and significance.
  5. Learn and Explore - Read fascinating facts, listen to audio notes, view high-quality photos, and discover the stories behind each specimen.
  6. Earn Badges - Complete collection challenges and earn achievement badges as you expand your virtual mineral collection.

Educational Benefits

SpecimenPro Events are designed with education at their core, providing numerous learning opportunities for participants of all ages.

Science Education

Learn about mineral classification, crystal structures, chemical compositions, and geological processes through interactive content designed by educators and geologists.

Critical Thinking

Develop observational skills and scientific thinking as you identify specimens, understand their properties, and make connections between different geological concepts.

Digital Literacy

Enhance technology skills while using mobile apps, QR codes, and digital resources in a real-world context.

Gain appreciation for Earth's natural resources and the importance of geological conservation and responsible specimen collection.
